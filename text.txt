Bangladesh Bank, the central bank and apex regulatory body for the
country's monetary and financial system, was established in Dhaka as a
body corporate vide the Bangladesh Bank Order, 1972 (P.O. No. 127 of 1972)
with effect from 16th December, 1971. At present it has ten offices
located at Motijheel, Sadarghat, Chittagong, Khulna, Bogra, Rajshahi,
Sylhet, Barisal, Rangpur and Mymensingh in Bangladesh; total manpower
stood at 5807 (officials 3981, subordinate staff 1826) as on March 31,2015
